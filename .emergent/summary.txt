Project Name: Duelo

1.  **Vision &amp; Design:** Create a competitive multiplayer quiz app named Duelo. The aesthetic should be 'Dark Mode Premium', inspired by QuizUp (2014). 2.  **Authentication**: Implement a hybrid system:
    *   A 'Guest' mode with a mandatory unique username.
    *   Progression saving via Sign in with Apple, Google Social Login, and Email/Password. A guest must be able to link their account later.
3.  **Game Loop:**
    *   **Matchmaking:** A radar animation screen to find an opponent. After 5 seconds, automatically connect to a 'Bot'. Use a hidden MMR score for balanced matchmaking. Display player win streaks on the versus screen.
    *   **The Duel:** 7 questions per match. A central 10-second circular timer.
    *   **Scoring:** Speed-based, from 20 points (instant) down to 10 points. 0 for wrong answers.
    *   **Feedback:** Haptic vibration and immediate Green/Red color feedback on answer selection.

        *   5 wins:  XP/match + ⚡ badge.
        *   10+ wins:  XP/match + Glow effect on the username.
    *   **Leaderboards:**
        *   Based on cumulative XP per category.
        *   Two views: 'All-Time' and 'Seasonal' (resets monthly).
